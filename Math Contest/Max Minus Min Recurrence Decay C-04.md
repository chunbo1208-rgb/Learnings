# Problem Statement
Let $\{a_n\}$ be a sequence of positive real numbers satisfying
$$a_n=\max_{1\le i\le 2025} a_{n-i}-\min_{1\le i\le 2025} a_{n-i},\qquad \forall n>2025.$$
Prove that there exists $M>0$ such that
$$a_n<\frac{1}{1404},\qquad \forall n>M.$$
