# Problem Statement
Let $\omega$ be a primitive $n$-th root of unity, and let $a_1,a_2,\dots,a_n$ be given complex numbers, of which at least $p\ge 1$ are nonzero. Define
$$b_k=\sum_{i=1}^n a_i\omega^{ki}.$$
Prove that among $b_1,b_2,\dots,b_n$, at least $n/p$ are nonzero.
