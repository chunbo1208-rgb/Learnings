# Problem Statement
Prove the following statements.

1. Let $p$ be a prime. Among any $2p-1$ integers, there exist $p$ whose sum is divisible by $p$.
2. Let $n$ be a positive integer. Among any $2n-1$ integers, there exist $n$ whose sum is divisible by $n$.
