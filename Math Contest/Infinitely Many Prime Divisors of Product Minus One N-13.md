# Problem Statement
Let $\{a_n\}_{n\ge 1}$ be a sequence of positive integers such that every prime greater than $2024$ divides some term of the sequence. Define
$$b_n=a_1a_2\cdots a_n-1\qquad (n\in\mathbb Z^+).$$
Prove that the sequence $\{b_n\}_{n\ge 1}$ has infinitely many prime divisors.
