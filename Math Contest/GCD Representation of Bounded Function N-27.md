# Problem Statement
Let $f:\mathbb Z\to\{1,2,3,\dots,10^{100}\}$ satisfy
$$\gcd(f(x),f(y))=\gcd(f(x),x-y)$$
for all integers $x,y$. Prove that there exist positive integers $m$ and $n$ such that
$$f(x)=\gcd(m+x,n)$$
for every integer $x$.
