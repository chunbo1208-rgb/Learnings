# Problem Statement
Suppose $a_1,a_2,\dots,a_n\in(-1,1)$. Prove that
$$\prod_{1\le i,j\le n}\frac{1+a_ia_j}{1-a_ia_j}\ge 1.$$
Find the necessary and sufficient condition for equality.
