# Problem Statement
Let real numbers $x,y,z$ satisfy
$$x^2+y^2+z^2=1.$$
Prove that
$$x^3-x(y^2+z^2+yz)+yz(y+z)\le 1.$$
Determine all triples $(x,y,z)$ for which equality holds.
