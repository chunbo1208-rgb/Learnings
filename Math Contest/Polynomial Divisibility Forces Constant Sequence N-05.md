# Problem Statement
Let $P(x)$ be a nonconstant polynomial with integer coefficients and $P(0)\ne 0$. Let $a_1,a_2,\dots$ be an infinite sequence of integers such that for all distinct positive integers $i,j$,
$$P(i-j)\mid a_i-a_j.$$
Prove that the sequence $a_1,a_2,\dots$ must be constant.
