# Problem Statement
An infinite strictly increasing sequence $a_n$ is called **centered** if for every positive integer $n$, the arithmetic mean of the first $a_n$ terms of the sequence equals $a_n$. Prove that there exists a sequence $b_1<b_2<\cdots$ such that for every centered sequence $a_n$, there are infinitely many $n$ with $a_n=b_n$.
