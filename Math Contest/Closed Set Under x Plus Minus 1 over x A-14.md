# Problem Statement
Determine all positive integers $n$ such that there exist distinct positive real numbers $a_1,\dots,a_n$ satisfying
$$\{a_i+(-1)^i a_i^{-1}\mid 1\le i\le n\}=\{a_i\mid 1\le i\le n\}.$$
