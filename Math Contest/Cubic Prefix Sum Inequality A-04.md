# Problem Statement
Let $n\ge 2$ be an integer, and let $a_1,a_2,\dots,a_n$ be positive real numbers with sum $1$. Prove that
$$\sum_{k=1}^n \frac{a_k}{1-a_k}(a_1+a_2+\cdots+a_{k-1})^2<\frac13.$$
