# Problem Statement
Given a positive integer $n$, determine the largest integer $c_n$ such that there exist a polynomial $f(x)$ of degree $n$ with rational coefficients, an irrational number $\alpha$, and $c_n$ distinct rational numbers $a_1,a_2,\dots,a_{c_n}$ satisfying that $f(\alpha+a_i)$ is rational for every $1\le i\le c_n$.
