# Problem Statement
Let $\mathbb Q^*$ be the set of all nonzero rational numbers. Suppose a surjective map $f:\mathbb Q^*\to\mathbb Q^*$ satisfies
$$\bigl(f(x)+f(y)\bigr)f(x+y)=f(xy),\qquad \forall x+y\ne 0.$$
Determine all such maps $f$.
