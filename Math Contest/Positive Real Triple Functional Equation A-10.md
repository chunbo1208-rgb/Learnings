# Problem Statement
Determine all functions $f:(0,\infty)\to(0,\infty)$ such that for all $x,y>0$,
$$f\bigl(f(f(xy))+x^2\bigr)=f(y)\bigl(f(x)-f(x+y)\bigr).$$
