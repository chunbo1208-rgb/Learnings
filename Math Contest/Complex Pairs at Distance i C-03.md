# Problem Statement
There are $777$ distinct complex numbers written on a blackboard. Exactly $760$ unordered pairs $(a,b)$ among them satisfy
$$a^2+b^2+1=2ab.$$
Prove that there exist two numbers $c,d$ on the blackboard such that
$$c^2+d^2+2025=2cd.$$
