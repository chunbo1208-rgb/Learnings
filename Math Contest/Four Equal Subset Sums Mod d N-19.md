# Problem Statement
Let $n\ge 3$ be an integer. Determine the largest positive integer $d$ with the following property: for every set $S$ of $n$ integers, there always exist four distinct nonempty subsets of $S$ (not necessarily disjoint) whose sums are all divisible by $d$.
