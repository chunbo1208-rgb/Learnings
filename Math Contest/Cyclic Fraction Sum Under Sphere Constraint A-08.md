# Problem Statement
Let $a,b,c,d\in(0,1)$ satisfy
$$a^2+b^2+c^2+d^2=3.$$
Prove that
$$\frac{1-a^2}{b+c}+\frac{1-b^2}{c+d}+\frac{1-c^2}{d+a}+\frac{1-d^2}{a+b}<\frac23.$$
