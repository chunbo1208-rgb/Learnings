# Problem Statement
Define $b_0=0$, and for $n\ge 1$ define
$$b_{n+1}=2b_n^2+b_n+1.$$
Prove that $b_{2k+1}-2b_{2k}$ is divisible by $2^{2k+2}$ but not divisible by $2^{2k+3}$.
