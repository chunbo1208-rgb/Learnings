# Problem Statement
Let $m,n$ be positive integers with $m\le n$. Prove that
$$\left|\sum_{k=m}^n \left(\cos\frac{k^2\pi}{2n}+i\sin\frac{k^2\pi}{2n}\right)\right|\le \frac{n}{m}.$$
