# Problem Statement
Call a positive integer $n$ **lonely** if for every pair of nonnegative integers $a,b$ with $a+b=n$, at least one of the decimal representations of $a$ and $b$ contains the digit $1$. Determine the number of lonely positive integers less than $10^{2026}$.
