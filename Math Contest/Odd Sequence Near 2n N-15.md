# Problem Statement
Let $a_1,a_2,\dots$ be positive odd integers such that for every positive integer $n$, the term $a_{n+2}$ is the smallest positive odd integer greater than $a_{n+1}$ and relatively prime to $a_n$. Prove that there exists a positive real number $C$ such that
$$|a_n-2n|\le C$$
for every positive integer $n$.
