# Problem Statement
Prove that there exists an integer $M\ge 3$ such that for every integer $n\ge M$ and any positive integers $a<b<c\le n$,
$$\gcd(a+b+c,\,ab+bc+ca,\,abc)<3n-\sqrt{3n}-2^{2026}.$$
